Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vastar.ai?

Vastar.ai is an All-In-One AI Productivity Partner designed to help professionals, students, and creators complete their tasks more efficiently. It integrates leading AI models like GPT-4o, Gemini, and Claude into one platform with features for writing, document analysis, podcasting, and more.

Who is Vastar.ai for?

Vastar.ai is built for a wide range of users in Indonesia, including professionals who need to draft reports, students working on assignments, and content creators developing new material. Essentially, it's for anyone looking to boost their productivity with AI.

Can I use my own documents with the AI?

Absolutely. Our Document Analysis (RAG) feature allows you to upload your files (like PDF, DOCX) and interact with them. You can ask questions about the content, generate summaries, create FAQs, and extract key information securely.

What happens if I exceed my monthly token quota?

We have a Fair Usage Policy in place. Depending on your plan, exceeding the quota may lead to reduced speeds or the option to purchase additional token packs. The PAYG (Pay-As-You-Go) plan is also available for users with variable or high-volume needs.
